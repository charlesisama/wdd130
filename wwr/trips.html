Our Most Popular Trips

Family Rapids – Beginner Level

Perfect for families with children or first-time rafters. This gentle 2-hour trip takes you through calm waters with occasional splashes. Equipment included: helmet, paddle, life jacket, and safety briefing.

Thunder Run – Intermediate Level

Experience exciting class III–IV rapids with expert guides. This 4-hour adventure includes scenic river views, moderate drops, and a mid-trip break. Recommended for guests with some rafting experience.

Raging Gorge – Expert Level

A full-day challenge through intense class IV–V rapids. Designed for adrenaline seekers who want the ultimate rafting experience. Includes professional gear, lunch, and rescue staff.
